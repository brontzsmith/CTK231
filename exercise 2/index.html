This poster's image suggests it comes prior to World Ward II. It has the phrase "Federal Arts Project" lending the idea it is a part of the WPA. The WPA or Works Progress Administration was program brounght to fruition in the time of Franklin Delano Roosevelt. This agency looked to hire people in an attempt to encourage the economy, end joblessness, and fight homelessness. The Federal Arts Project was a section of the WPA, hiring such notables as Orson Wells and Arthur Miller.
